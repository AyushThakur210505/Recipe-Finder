Chow Mein | veg Chow Mein Recipe

Chow mein is a popular Chinese dish of stir fried noodles with mix vegetables, soy sauce, aromatics and spices. My street style veg chowmein recipe is a delightful Indo-Chinese fusion. This vegan dish is a savory masterpiece of smooth, velvety noodles and oodles of crunchy veggies.

Serve 4


50 mintues


calories / 100g

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g chow mein noodles (or any other noodles of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced (you can use any color)
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 cups cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

METHOD

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced onion, bell pepper, carrot, and shredded cabbage to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the bean sprouts to the skillet and stir-fry for another minute.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet along with the soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Toss everything together until the noodles and ingredients are well combined and heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with chopped spring onions and sesame seeds if desired.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious vegetarian chow mein!

STORAGE

  1. Refrigerate in an airtight container for 3-4 days.
  2. Freeze in portions for up to 2-3 months.

SERVING

  1. Reheat in microwave or stovetop until warmed through.
  2. Garnish with fresh herbs or sesame seeds.
  3. Serve with soy sauce or chili sauce.
  4. Pair with other Chinese dishes for a complete meal.
